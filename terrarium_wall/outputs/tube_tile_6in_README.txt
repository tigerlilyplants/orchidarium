6 inch tessellating smooth tube coral tile
==========================================

Dimensions: 152.4 mm x 152.4 mm base tile, 6.35 mm base, 38.1 mm max height.
Tube bases are constrained to the plate; only the upper growth leans/flares past edges.
Some upper tube geometry intentionally overhangs the base edges so repeated tiles do not leave an empty border.
This version uses crowded flared tube tops to hide most of the plate in top view.
The underside has eight 10.2 mm square blind ports, 4.3 mm deep.
Use the straight connector under side-by-side seams and the corner connector where four tiles meet.

Color/STL handling: STL does not reliably store color. Import the three tile color STLs together without moving them, then assign white, beige, and orange materials.

Files:
- tube_tile_6in_white.stl: 11 white tubes
- tube_tile_6in_beige.stl: beige base plus 110 beige tubes
- tube_tile_6in_orange.stl: 27 orange tubes
- tube_tile_6in_combined_reference.stl: all tile geometry merged for preview
- tube_tile_straight_snap_connector.stl: two-stud seam connector
- tube_tile_corner_snap_connector.stl: four-stud corner connector
- tube_tile_6in_tessellation_preview.svg: 3x3 repeated top-view layout

Connector fit note: studs are 9.55 mm for 10.2 mm ports, with a small retention rib. Scale connectors down 1-2% if your printer runs tight.
