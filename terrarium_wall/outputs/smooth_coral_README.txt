Smooth coral relief STL set
===========================

Generated from the provided surface reference as a stylized smooth-coral relief, not a direct scan.
The model is 120 mm x 120 mm, with a 4 mm beige base and raised smooth coral cups.

Color/STL handling:
- STL files do not reliably preserve color.
- Import the three color STLs together into your slicer without moving them.
- Assign filaments/materials: white, beige, and orange.
- smooth_coral_combined_reference.stl is included only as a one-piece preview/reference.

Files:
- smooth_coral_white.stl: 4 white coral cells
- smooth_coral_beige.stl: beige base plus 73 beige coral cells
- smooth_coral_orange.stl: 32 orange coral cells
- smooth_coral_combined_reference.stl: all geometry merged
